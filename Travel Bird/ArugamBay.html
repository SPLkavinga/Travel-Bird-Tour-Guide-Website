ArugamBay Tour


The Best Place To Get unforgettable Experience in in Nestled on the eastern coast of Sri Lanka.


Receive an early-morning pickup from your hotel in Arugam Bay, and embark on a journey to discover the hidden gems of this coastal paradise. Nestled on Sri Lanka's eastern shore, Arugam Bay beckons travelers with its captivating blend of pristine beaches and vibrant local culture. Set against a backdrop of swaying palm trees and the azure Indian Ocean, Arugam Bay offers an idyllic escape for surfers and beach lovers alike. Its charming streets are lined with lively eateries, surf shops, and welcoming locals eager to share the area's rich heritage. Visitors can ride the waves at the renowned surf spots, explore the nearby Pottuvil Lagoon teeming with wildlife, or simply relax on the golden sands. As the sun sets, Arugam Bay transforms into a laid-back haven, where the gentle sounds of the ocean and the distant laughter of beach bonfires create an atmosphere of pure relaxation.
